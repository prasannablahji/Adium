Marketing Strategy and Process

Brand Recognition

We strive to be well known as a personal cloud-based workspace where anyone with an internet connection can perform all of their digital work without any computational limits. We would also bring light to the fact that users can also store all their digital data without any storage limits, and access their workspace across devices, seamlessly.

Our Vision

Our ultimate goal is to empower people to focus on their work from anywhere on the world from any internet enabled device. Consequently, this would result in less time wasted on purchasing and setting up hardware and software.
